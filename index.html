Welcome

Welcome to my personal website and blog. Currently, I’m a PhD student at City University London working on fluid-structure interaction problems (aeroelasticity). I use mainly open source "FREE" tools in my research, in particular, OpenFOAM, ParaView, Gnuplot , python, Salome and FreeCAD. I will share on my blog some tips and ideas about these tools with a special focus on OpenFOAM.
